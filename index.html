About Me

I'm currently pursuing my Masters in Mechanical Engineering and Robotics at Oregon State, where I research terrain sensing systems for legged robotics.

My bachelors degree is in Mechanical Engineering, from Arizona State. While there, I dedicated much of my time to Formula SAE, a collegiate race-car competition. My work involved full-stack electronic hardware, including mechanical and suspension components, enclosures, wiring harnesses, PCBs, and embedded software. I'm passionate about hardware/software integration, and solving problems hands-on. 

Beyond engineering, I'm an avid outdoorsman and amateur photographer, and have my favorite works showcased in a dedicated section.
